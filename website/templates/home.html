Bahia - The Safest, Simplest P2P NFT Trading Platform Ever.

For Traders, By Traders

We set out to create the most straight-forward method of buying and selling NFTs between peers. Our goal was and continues to be making the NFT trade as simple, secure, and efficient as possible.

Don't Pay for Market Makers

Exchanges charge 2.5% for creating a market for you, and collections charge an additional 5+% on platforms like OpenSea and LooksRare. Bahia is for sellers that can find their own buyers, charging only 1% to keep improving our platform for you.

How Bahia Works

Buy

  1. Enter Trade Address
  2. Click Buy
  3. Buyer receives NFT, Seller receives ETH

Sell

  1. Select NFT
  2. Set Purchase Terms
  3. Get Trade Address

Security

Anti-Scam

No Links. Ever. Seriously though, we never want a user to be scammed, so we have made the purchase process watertight to keep users safe. This includes getting rid of links, ERC20 asset trades, and custodial/escrow smart contracts.

Non-Custodial

No escrow accounts that force you to transfer your NFTs before sale. The NFT will never leave your wallet until the transaction is complete, and you have received the ETH.

Get Paid in ETH

Sellers on Bahia only accept ETH. No WETH, no shitcoins, no scams. As soon as a buyer purchases the NFT, the ETH goes straight to the seller, and the NFT goes straight to the buyer.

Complete Transparency

We provide access to everything a buyer should know with a single click. This includes the collection address, id, content, and even LooksRare link to the collection, so you can verify that the NFT is exactly what you expect.

What is Bahia?
Bahia is a P2P NFT trading platform where holders can sell directly to buyers without interacting with an exchange. For now, that means that sellers can sell one NFT at a time for a specified ETH price. Over time, we will implement features to trade NFTs and transact in batches.
Bahia vs. OpenSea
OpenSea charges much higher fees to create full exchanges, complete with many features like soliciting bids and viewing entire collections of NFTs. This assists some sellers in finding buyers, but it is not always necessary. For example, community members and influencers often find their own buyers, which negates any need for a full exchange. Bahia allows you to transact safely with lower fees, as we are not charging you for exchange-related activities (e.g. soliciting bids, listing within a collection with comparable NFTs).
How can Bahia charge less than OpenSea?LooksRare?
Bahia and OpenSea collect fees in the same manner. When a transaction occurs, it passes through a smart contract. This smart contract ensures that the both sides of the transaction (ETH & NFT) are fulfilled simultaneously. In the same transaction, the contract takes 1% of the ETH and retains it for the Bahia platform. Simply put, the smart contract pays the platform wallet a small fee mere milliseconds before delivering the ETH to the seller.
What fees do I pay?
To create a transaction, gas of approximately 0.0005-0.001 ETH is required. After the sale finishes and the seller is paid, a fee of 1% is paid to the platform for facilitating a safe transfer. There are no fees paid to artists or collections.
How do I trade on Bahia?
The seller picks an NFT to sell, sets the price, sets the expiration, and creates the trade. Once the trade is created, the seller must approve the contract (click the activate button) to sell the NFT, which is required for any ERC721 transaction (can be canceled at any time).

The buyer, after receiving the trade address from the seller (via discord, twitter, etc.), goes to bahia.io/buy and inputs the trade address. The buyer will see the NFT being offered, and can click the LOOKSRARE and ETHERSCAN buttons to ensure the NFT collection is verified. Once the buyer clicks buy (as long as they have the adequate amount of ethereum in their wallet), the NFT will arrive in their wallet, and the seller will receive the payment.
What if I don’t specify a buyer when selling an NFT?
If a seller doesn’t specify any buyer, anyone who visits the trade address via bahia.io/buy can purchase the NFT for the specified price.
What makes Bahia safe?
Bahia ensures that only ethereum can be sent, making it impossible to receive suspicious ERC20 assets.

NFTs stay in your wallet. This means that if our site goes down for any reason, your NFTs are still safe. In fact, you can cancel the transaction at any time via any ERC721 contract or by simply moving the NFT to a different wallet. We only provide a transaction for the buyer to accept. The buyer can only send the specified price, and we provide the buyer with LooksRare and Etherscan links to verify they will receive the correct NFT.

Our contract will never require that you approve access to all your NFTs, allowing maximum flexibility and security to sellers.

Sellers can cancel any transaction at any time.
Where do I find buyers for my NFTs?
Many discords have marketplace threads where you can post your NFT and an asking price. We recommend posting your NFT, the asking price, and the Bahia trade address after you’ve opened the trade. You never need to send a link. If asked, buyers should all just be directed to the general dApp link: bahia.io/buy

In addition, we have a monitored discord thread where buyers and sellers can find one another safely.
What if I need help?
Feel free to consult our discord and ask any questions/voice any concerns. We will get to them as soon as possible, and ensure that you can resume transacting on Bahia with safety and ease.
