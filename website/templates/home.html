Good Morning

We waited. We listened to the market. We built our vision for NFTs from the ground up. The goal: make trading simple, easy, and accessible for all.

Our Vision

Two devs and an NFT enthusiast started Bahia out of sheer willpower. Naturally, everyone wants revolutionary technology to succeed. However, the market has consistently priced out early adopters or introduced too much friction for people to profit in the long-run. As a result, we built two solutions. Pools let investors trustlessly pool capital to purchase blue-chip NFTs. Bartering lets peers engage in trading with only 1% total fees.
